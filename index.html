About

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at Middle East Technical University in Ankara, Turkey — one of the country's leading technical institutions.

My focus lies at the intersection of numerical simulation and physical engineering: running CFD analyses on fluid systems, building FEA models for structural problems, and developing NDT probes for flaw detection. I work with both open-source tools like OpenFOAM and ElmerFEM and industry-standard software like Ansys and SolidWorks.

I've applied this work in industry through internships at Beko Corporate (Quality Assurance) and Mim Engineering (structural steel production) — gaining hands-on experience in manufacturing processes and measurement systems.
